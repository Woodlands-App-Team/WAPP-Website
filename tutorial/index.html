Tutorial

Select a page and we'll give you helpful information and tips as to how that page works and what you can do!

Announcements & Events

The announcements and events page is the "main" page and features announcements (with the background colour grey) and upcoming events (with the background colour blue). You can also search for and filter announcements and events. Tapping select announcements and events will show the expanded text of it.

The announcements & events page.

A collapsed announcement card (notice the grey background).

An expanded announcement card. Tap the card to toggle expansion.

A collapsed event card (notice the blue background).

An expanded event card. Tap the card to toggle expansion.

Cafeteria

The cafeteria page contains a list of daily specials for each day and a menu, along with each food item's price and any allergens it contains.

The cafeteria page.

A menu card.

A flipped menu card (tap a card to toggle flip).

Song Requests

The song requests page allows students to upvote and view requested songs, and request any song they'd like to be played on the school speakers in the morning!

The song requests page. Tap a song's card to "upvote" it. You'll see a checkmark where the outlined circle is if it's upvoted. Tap "+ Request Song" to request a song of your own!

You can request a song here; this uses Spotify's search!

Clubs & Teams

You can view the clubs here at The Woodlands and get more detailed info about the club, such as meeting time, etc.

The club page.

A specific club's card. Tap the card to view more details about the club. Tap the notification icon at the top-right to toggle notifications for that club.

A specific club's page. You can read about the club, see grade restrictions, and get meeting times!
